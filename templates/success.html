How it can help Businesses

This project focuses on extraction of final bill-amount and due date from utility bills like telephone bills, electricity bills,etc. Optical Character Recognition deals with the problem of recognizing all kinds of different characters. Both handwritten and printed characters can be recognized and converted into machine-readable, digital data format.

We can help you to grow your business

Businesses that employ OCR capabilities save time and resources that would otherwise be necessary to manage unsearchable data. OCR-processed textual information can be used by businesses for various purposes such as machine learning or statistical analysis. It eliminates manual data entry, helps process more data faster and with fewer resources, reduces errors, improves productivity
